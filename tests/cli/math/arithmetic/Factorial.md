# `Factorial`

### `Factorial[0]`

The factorial of 0 is 1 by definition.

```scrut
$ wo 'Factorial[0]'
1
```


### `Factorial[1]`

The factorial of 1 is 1.

```scrut
$ wo 'Factorial[1]'
1
```


### `Factorial[5]`

The factorial of 5 is 120 (5! = 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1).

```scrut
$ wo 'Factorial[5]'
120
```
