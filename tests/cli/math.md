# Math

Woxi implements a large subset of the Wolfram Language's numeric,
symbolic, and linear-algebraic functions. This section is split into
topical pages — each with scrut-tested examples that are verified
against `wolframscript`.

- [Arithmetic](math/arithmetic.md) — `Plus`, `Times`, `Power`, `Mod`, `Max`, `Min`, `Factorial`, `GCD`, …
- [Elementary Functions](math/elementary.md) — `Sin`, `Cos`, `Log`, `Exp`, hyperbolic, and inverse trig.
- [Number Theory & Combinatorics](math/number_theory.md) — `Prime`, `Fibonacci`, `Binomial`, `Divisors`, …
- [Complex Numbers & Predicates](math/complex.md) — `Re`, `Im`, `Conjugate`, `NumberQ`, `NumericQ`, …
- [Bit Operations](math/bits.md) — `BitAnd`, `BitOr`, `BitShift*`, …
- [Special Functions](math/special.md) — `Gamma`, `Zeta`, `Bessel*`, `Erf`, `Elliptic*`, …
- [Linear Algebra](math/linear_algebra.md) — `Dot`, `Det`, `Inverse`, `Eigenvalues`, `LinearSolve`, …
- [Polynomials](math/polynomials.md) — `PolynomialGCD`, `Resultant`, `Coefficient`, `Exponent`, …
- [Algebraic Manipulation](math/algebraic.md) — `Expand`, `Factor`, `Simplify`, `Cancel`, `Apart`, `Together`, …
- [Utility](math/utility.md) — `Unitize`, `UnitStep`, `Reap`/`Sow`, `RandomInteger`, `MemberQ`.
