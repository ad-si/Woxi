# List Operations

Woxi implements a comprehensive subset of the Wolfram Language's list
functions. The material has been split into topical sub-pages:

- [Basics](list/basics.md) — `Length`, `First`, `Last`, `Rest`, `Most`, `Part`, `Take`, `Drop`, `Append`, `Prepend`, `Reverse`, `Range`, `Array`, `Table`, `Join`, `Sort`, `Dimensions`, …
- [Transforming Lists](list/transform.md) — `Map`, `MapAt`, `MapIndexed`, `Scan`, `Apply`, `Thread`, `Flatten`, `ArrayFlatten`, `Transpose`, `Outer`, `Inner`, `Pick`, `Composition`, …
- [Sorting and Ordering](list/sort_order.md) — `Sort`, `SortBy`, `ReverseSort`, `Ordering`, `OrderedQ`, `MaximalBy`, `MinimalBy`, `Permutations`, `Signature`, …
- [Searching and Filtering](list/search.md) — `Position`, `Count`, `Counts`, `Cases`, `FirstCase`, `Select`, `SelectFirst`, `DeleteCases`, `DeleteDuplicates`, `Tally`, `Commonest`, `FreeQ`, …
- [Set Operations](list/sets.md) — `Union`, `Intersection`, `Complement`, `Subsets`, `Subsequences`, `ContainsAll`, `DisjointQ`, `MinMax`, …
- [Grouping and Partitioning](list/grouping.md) — `Partition`, `Split`, `Gather`, `GatherBy`, `GroupBy`, `BinCounts`, `TakeList`, …
- [Statistics and Summaries](list/statistics.md) — `Mean`, `Median`, `Total`, `Product`, `Accumulate`, `Differences`, `Ratios`.
