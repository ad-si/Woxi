# Dates & Time

Woxi implements the most common date/time functions from the
Wolfram Language. Dates can be specified either as 3-element lists
`{year, month, day}` or as `DateObject[...]`.

- [`DateString`](dates/DateString.md)
- [`Now`](dates/Now.md)
- [`Today`](dates/Today.md)
- [`DateObject`](dates/DateObject.md)
- [`DayName`](dates/DayName.md)
- [`LeapYearQ`](dates/LeapYearQ.md)
- [`AbsoluteTiming`](dates/AbsoluteTiming.md)
- [`Timing`](dates/Timing.md)
